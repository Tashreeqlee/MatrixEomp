About Us

Matrix Performance founded by Tashreeq Lee started as a childhood dream for him where all he knew was cars. His journey started all the way in Cape Town where street racing and car culture are very special. The dream started by Tashreeq Lee, was then turned to reality in 2020 which was the peak year of Covid. He decided to put his mind to work and started tuning his friends cars using his software and coding skills which then led to his friends recommending their friends and since then he has been doing it regularly and have now branched out into selling accessories for all kingds of vehicles.

Matrix Performance Tuning specializes in high-quality exhaust systems that are designed to enhance the performance of your vehicle and also suiting the needs of customers to accessorise their vehicle.

We offer a wide range of accessories for a variety of vehicles, including cars, trucks, and SUVs. Our team of experts is dedicated to providing exceptional customer service and ensuring that you get your needs sorted for your vehicle.

Whether you're looking for a performance boost or simply want to upgrade the look of your car, Matrix Performance has the perfect solution for you.
